A money mindset is your collection of subconscious beliefs, attitudes,
and assumptions about money, shaped by your upbringing and experiences, that dictates how you earn, spend and manage it.
often falling into scarcity (fear, lack) or abundance (opportunity, security) categories.
A positive mindset fostering healthy habits for financial success.
It acts as a filter, influencing your financial confidence and whether you feel in control or stuck in debt cycles.
